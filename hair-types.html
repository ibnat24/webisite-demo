Understanding Hair Types

Hair types are typically categorized into four major groups: straight (Type 1), wavy (Type 2), curly (Type 3), and coily/kinky (Type 4). Each type requires different care methods and products.

  • Type 1: Straight – Smooth and shiny, tends to get oily quickly.
  • Type 2: Wavy – Has a slight wave, can frizz, benefits from light styling products.
  • Type 3: Curly – Defined curls, needs hydration and curl-defining creams.
  • Type 4: Coily/Kinky – Tight curls or coils, prone to dryness, requires deep conditioning.
