About This Website

Hairstyle and More is a student project designed to help users understand their hair type and find personalized styling tips and haircut recommendations. Whether you have straight, wavy, curly, or coily hair, our site offers insights and style suggestions tailored to your routine and preferences.

This website was created as part of the MSE 543 course at the University of Waterloo to demonstrate user-centered design and usability testing principles. All content is for educational purposes only.

Have feedback or ideas for improvement? We'd love to hear from you! Reach out through the course discussion board or contact the student developer directly.
